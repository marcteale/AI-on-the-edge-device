Step 1 / 7: Adjust Focus and Verify Flashlight

First, find a proper mounting position for your device. You may have to have to adjust the camera lens focus to get a sharp, crisp image. Use the live stream with LED flash to complete this step. Details on camera lens adjustment can be found here: Focus Adjustment
Verify that reflections from the LED do not interfere with the readability of data areas. External LEDs are supported if proper illumination cannot be obtained using the onboard LED. See the documentation for details. Installation Of External LEDs

The flash intensity defaults to 50%, but can be changed in the next step. You can return to this screen after making adjustments.
This live stream can also be called at any time after setup is complete.
